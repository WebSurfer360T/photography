Photography

“When words become unclear, I shall focus with photographs. When images become inadequate, I shall be content with silence.”

— Ansel Adams

Photographs evoke nostalgia. They tell the story that we don't always speak. They're the legacy we leave behind, offering a way to share our stories with generations to come.



THE IMPORTANCE

Photography used to be an expensive hobby. With the advent of smartphones, it's even more empowering to take a crack at shooting our own images. We can use photos to express ourselves and communicate with others. Sharing our favorite photos has become effortless, from uploading to social media, to creating albums for print, they're simple to share in both digital and traditional ways.

One of my favorite things to take photos of are sunsets and sunrises. Its almost as if its artwork is spread across the sky for us to see and enjoy. My dream is to own my own photography business and see where it takes me!
